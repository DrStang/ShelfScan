About Data Deletion

When you delete your ShelfScan account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Account information: Your email address and authentication credentials
  • Scan history: All your book scans and their results
  • Reading list: Your imported Goodreads library data
  • Usage data: App preferences and settings

Data Retention

After deletion:

  • All data is deleted immediately and permanently
  • This action cannot be undone
  • No backup copies are retained
  • Aggregate analytics (no personal info) may be retained for service improvement
